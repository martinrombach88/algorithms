Digits from 0 to 9 backwards
Write a program that prints numbers from 0 to 9, but in the backward order.
All numbers must be in the same line.
Separate them by a space.

The output should be the following:

9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 1 0
It is possible to use numbers, strings or characters to solve this task. Choose any way to solve this task.
